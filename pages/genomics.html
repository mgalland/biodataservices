Genomic & omics analyses

Helping you to get access to the latest development in Next-Generation Sequencing technologies as well as in various "omics" techniques. Some examples include:

  • Transcriptomics through RNA-Seq: Transcriptomics through RNA-Seq (messenger RNA, small RNAs).
  • Mapping-by-Sequencing: finding the genomic regions related to a trait of interest based on Next-Generation Sequencing (i.e. Genotyping-by-Sequencing).
  • Analysis of "omics" data: finding biomarkers related to a phenotype of interest (disease resistance, abiotic stress resistance, etc.).
